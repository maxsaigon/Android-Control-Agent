# Cross-Platform Intelligence Rules

## 1. Platform Timing Windows (Vietnam timezone - UTC+7)

| Platform | Peak Hours | Off-Peak | Avoid |
|----------|-----------|----------|-------|
| TikTok | 19:00-23:00 | 12:00-14:00 | 02:00-06:00 |
| Facebook | 08:00-10:00, 18:00-20:00 | 12:00-14:00 | 00:00-05:00 |
| Instagram | 11:00-13:00, 19:00-21:00 | 15:00-17:00 | 01:00-06:00 |
| YouTube | 14:00-17:00, 20:00-22:00 | 10:00-12:00 | 00:00-07:00 |

## 2. Platform Switching Rules

- **Minimum gap**: 5 minutes between switching platforms on same device
- **Maximum platforms/day**: 3 per device (avoid behavioral fingerprinting)
- **Session order**: Start with most important platform for campaign goal
- **Cooldown**: After any platform session, browse something neutral (browser/other)

## 3. Persona Consistency

- Comment style phải nhất quán across platforms
- Cùng niche interest (food → food trên tất cả platforms)
- Language consistency (nếu comment VN trên TikTok → cũng VN trên FB)
- Avoid follow cho competitor trên platform A nhưng follow brand trên platform B

## 4. Trending Bridge

- Sound/hashtag trending TikTok → tìm same content trên Instagram Reels
- Viral Facebook post → engage related content trên YouTube
- Popular YouTube video → comment trên TikTok duet/reaction

## 5. Risk Distribution

- Đừng tập trung tất cả actions vào 1 platform
- Nếu 1 platform bị restrict → tăng activity trên platforms khác
- Monthly rotation: thay đổi primary platform focus
